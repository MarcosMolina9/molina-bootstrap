hero game

warriors is a online game which will place you
in the role of controlling a hero with his pet in order
to regain control of the kingdom of Ohm.

rarity stone

Rarity stones are stones that allow you to improve
the attributes of an item / pet. For this, it will be
necessary to purchase the stone and use it on the selected
object / pet. There will be a 49% chance that the stats will
improve, 49% that they will get worse and 2% that they will
stay the same.
